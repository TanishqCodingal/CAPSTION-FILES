BUILDING MAINTAINANCE

Building maintenance is the process of keeping a structure safe, functional, and in good condition through regular inspections, repairs, and preventive measures. It involves tasks such as cleaning, painting, plumbing, electrical work, and ensuring that heating, ventilation, and air-conditioning systems operate efficiently. Proper maintenance not only extends the lifespan of a building but also enhances the comfort and safety of its occupants. It helps prevent costly damages by addressing minor issues before they become major problems. In addition, well-maintained buildings contribute to energy efficiency, reduce environmental impact, and preserve property value over time.
